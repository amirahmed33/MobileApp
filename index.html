Mobile applications (also known as mobile apps) are software programs developed for mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. They turn mobile devices into miniature powerhouses of function and fun. Some devices come preloaded with some mobile apps courtesy of their manufacturers or the mobile service providers with which they're associated (for example, Verizon, T-Mobile, etc.), but many more apps are available through device-specific app stores.
